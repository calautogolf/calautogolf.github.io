About Us


Berkeley Unmanned Driving and Sensing is an autonomous driving project affiliated with Robotics@Berkeley. We are a team of nine Berkeley students with backgrounds in mechanical, electrical, and software engineering. We are currently working on fitting a golfcart with computer vision and sensors in hopes that it can one day navigate the UC Berkeley campus independent of human input. If you would like to join us, fill out this form and we will contact you.

RC Car hack

As a preliminary step to the autonomous golf cart, we are hacking an RC car in the aims of building a small scale autonomous vehicle.
