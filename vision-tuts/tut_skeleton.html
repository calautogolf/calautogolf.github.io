01 - Neural Networks

The basis of the computer vision paradigm is the concept of machine learning. Computer vision has been heavily reliant on a set of algorithms known as neural networks (such as convolutional and deep nets). At current computer vision levels, these algorithms appear extremely daunting. However, starting with basic neural networks can provide you an intuition into how these algorithms work.

Michael Nielsen has an excellent textbook on neural networks the simplifies much of the complicated mathematics of neural networks into a graspable form.
