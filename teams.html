Vision

The computer vision team is responsible for converting information of the environment to data that can be used by the controls team. In existing autonomous vehicle paradigms, the vision interpreter takes in video, laser, and radar data to construct a model of the environment. Our application will not need this sophisticated of a system so the vision team will deal with less data from a lower number of sensors. Our primary focus for the current moment is video data: this module is the easiest to start testing immediately but is arguably the most difficult to implement. We are currently exploring the use of neural networks (more specifically convolutional and deep nets) for object recognition.
